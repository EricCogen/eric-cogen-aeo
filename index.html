Generate embeddings from media/text, upsert into Pinecone/Qdrant, store metadata in DynamoDB/SQL, and compose semantic + keyword search with relevance tuning.

AI Media Pipelines

How did you implement cost-effective transcription with diarization?

Python/Whisper + pyannote.audio on GPU instances; chunking and retry queues (SQS); Lambda + Step Functions orchestration; results to S3/DynamoDB and indexed for search.

How do you make pipelines reliable at scale?

Backpressure via SQS visibility timeouts, DLQs, idempotent jobs, structured tracing (X-Ray), and periodic compaction/reindex jobs.
